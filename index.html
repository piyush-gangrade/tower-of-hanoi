Tower of Hanoi

Steps: 0
Time: 00:00
Minimum steps: 7

How to Play

  • Only one disk can be drag at a time.
  • Each drag consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack. In other words, a disk can only be dragged if it is the uppermost disk on a stack.
  • No larger disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
